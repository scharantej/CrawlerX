Types of Crawlers

There are many different types of crawlers, each with its own strengths and weaknesses.

Some of the most common types of crawlers include:

  • General-purpose crawlers
  • Focused crawlers
  • Incremental crawlers

General-purpose crawlers crawl the entire web, while focused crawlers crawl a specific set of websites or pages.

Incremental crawlers only crawl new or updated content.
